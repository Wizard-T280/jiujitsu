The 1st 12 months of classes will be a Fundamentals Curriculum focusing on the basics of JiuJitsu, Wrestling and Self Defense. This is a 12 month cyclical program for beginners but all ranks are welcome to attend.

After attending 12 months of the Fundamentals, students will then be able to comprehend the basic movements of JiuJitsu, have a concept for dealing with an opponent throwing strikes and progress to more advanced classes.
